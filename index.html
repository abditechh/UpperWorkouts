💪 Upper Body Workout

🔥 Warm-Up

  • 5 min light cardio
  • Dynamic stretching + foam rolling

1. Barbell Overhead Press

  • Week 1: 2 × 4
  • Week 2: 3 × 4
  • Week 3: 4 × 4
  • Week 4: 2 × 4 (heavier)
  • Keep core tight
  • Bar moves straight overhead

2. Wide-Grip Pull-Ups

  • 3 × 6
  • Full stretch
  • Controlled tempo

3. Close-Grip Bench Press

  • Week 1: 2 × 10
  • Week 2: 2 × 11
  • Week 3: 2 × 12
  • Week 4: 2 × 10 (heavier)
  • Elbows tucked

4A. Seated Cable Row

  • 3 × 12

4B. Incline Dumbbell Lateral Raise

  • 3 × 15
  • Superset (minimal rest)

5A. Cable Crossover

  • 2 sets (10 low + 10 mid)

5B. Rope Face Pull

  • 2 × 20

6. Supinated Dumbbell Curl

  • 3 × 12
  • No swinging

⏱ Rest Times

  • Compound: 2–3 min
  • Isolation: 1–2 min

📈 Goal

  • Add reps first, then weight
  • Stay 1–2 reps shy of failure
